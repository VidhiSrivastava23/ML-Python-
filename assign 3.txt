<h1 style="color:white;text align:center;">WHAT SHOULD BE AQI</h1>
<p>The ideal Air Quality Index (AQI) for a city is between 0 and 100, indicating "Good" or "Satisfactory" air quality with little to no risk to health.The Air Quality Index (AQI) in Delhi is 394, which means the air quality is Hazardous right now.</p>

<h2 style="color:white;text align:center;">WHAT MEASURES CAN BE TAKEN </h2>
<p>Solutions to pollution include reducing vehicle use through walking, biking, or public transport, and improving vehicle efficiency. Other solutions involve conserving energy, reducing waste by recycling and reusing, and proper disposal of chemicals and trash. Industrial solutions involve using cleaner energy sources like solar and wind, and installing filters on industrial emissions. </p>
